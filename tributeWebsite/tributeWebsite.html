A Tribuite to Rich Piana

A body builder who inspired many people and who will live on forever

Here is a quick timeline on his life and achievments

  • Rich Piana was born in Glendale CA in the year 1970.
  • He first became obsessed with body building at the age of 6.
  • He started his first cycle at the age of 18 in 1998
  • Rich won his first title of Mr. Teen California in 1989
  • In 2014 Rich finally started to talk about the side effects he was experiencing
  • During his last years Rich focused on his brand, 5%, and his youtube channel
  • In 2017 Rich passed away from steroids and substance abuse
